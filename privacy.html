Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 25, 2026

Overview

YOLO Enterprise LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the yolo-enterprises.com website and related internal software systems. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate our internal systems. This may include phone numbers of individuals who are pre-authorized to receive automated system status alerts via SMS.

How We Use Your Information

Phone numbers enrolled in our SMS alert system are used solely for delivering automated operational notifications such as system status updates. We do not send marketing messages, promotional content, or any communication unrelated to system operations.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, lease, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or any other purpose. The above specifically includes text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Access to personal data is limited to the system administrator.

Data Retention

Phone numbers are retained only for as long as the recipient is actively enrolled in the alert system. Upon opt-out or removal, phone numbers are deleted promptly.

Your Rights

You may request removal of your personal information at any time by contacting admin@yolo-enterprises.com. SMS recipients may also reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at admin@yolo-enterprises.com.
