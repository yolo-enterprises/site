SMS Alert Policy

YOLO Enterprise LLC operates a low-volume automated alert system that delivers operational notifications via SMS to pre-authorized recipients.

Message content: System status alerts, operational notifications, and time-sensitive administrative messages. No marketing or promotional messages are sent.

Frequency: Message volume does not exceed a few messages per day.

Enrollment: Recipients are manually enrolled by the system administrator. Consent to receive messages is not a condition of any purchase or service. This service is not open to public subscription.

Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You may also contact admin@yolo-enterprises.com to be removed.

Help: Reply HELP for assistance, or email admin@yolo-enterprises.com.

Message and data rates may apply. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
